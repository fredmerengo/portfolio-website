Privacy & Cookies

We respect your privacy. This site uses a consent-controlled embedded map. The map is loaded only if you click "Load Map", and that preference is stored locally in your browser. The contact form posts to Formspree (a third-party service) — replace the form action with your Formspree form ID if you use it.

Data stored locally

  • mpc_map_consent — stores whether you allowed the embedded Google Map.
  • mpc_cookies_consent — stores site-wide cookie consent if you accepted cookies.

Contact form

Form submissions are sent to the configured service (Formspree). Email addresses entered are used to respond to your message; we do not share addresses publicly.
